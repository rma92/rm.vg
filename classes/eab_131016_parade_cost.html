An agreement to fund homecoming parade float

This is an agreement between the Engineering Advisory Board (herein EAB), Veteran's Student Organization (herein VSO), College Republicans, Council of International Students and Organizations (herein COISO), and Eaton Residential College (herein Eaton), pertaining to the funding
  1. VSO will pay for all applicable purchases.
  2. The other four orgs will give VSO one fifth of the total amount spent by VSO
  3. The maximum spending limit (for VSO) will be set to $500.
  4. The $300 refundable deposit for the float will be paid for by EAB.

Labour will be divided in an ad hoc manner, as determined appropriate.

The undersigned agree to be bound by this agreement, or any superseding agreement agreed upon by all parties involved.
